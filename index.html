Questions

Frequently Asked

How many career goals has Cristiano Ronaldo scored?
He has scored over 900 official senior career goals for club and country, making him the highest goalscorer in football history.
How many Ballon d'Or awards has he won?
Cristiano Ronaldo has won 5 Ballon d'Or trophies throughout his illustrious career (2008, 2013, 2014, 2016, 2017).
What clubs has Ronaldo played for?
Sporting CP, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, Manchester United (again), and currently Al Nassr FC in Saudi Arabia.
What is CR7's most iconic celebration?
The famous "SIUUU" — a spinning leap with arms spread wide, landing chest-out and shouting "SIUUU." Messi fans cannot relate.
Has Ronaldo won the World Cup?
No, but he won the UEFA Euro 2016 with Portugal — his most cherished international trophy. He also reached the 2006 World Cup semi-finals.
